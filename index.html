Data Automation

Full-Stack Data Cleaning App

I developed a full-stack Data Cleaning Application designed to automate the preparation of CSV, Excel, and TXT datasets. The application supports bulk folder uploads and direct Google Drive file links, making it highly versatile for corporate environments. I implemented an Automatic Duplicate Check that identified 320 duplicate rows from a 500-row dataset in seconds. Upon cleaning, the tool generated a refined output of 180 unique rows, ensuring complete data accuracy for downstream analysis. To enhance user experience, I integrated a KPI Builder and text transformation modules for instant data filtering and processing. This automation significantly reduces the manual labor required for data prep, allowing analysts to focus on insight generation rather than formatting.

Amazon Store Dashboard

I created an end-to-end retail dashboard tracking 2M in sales and 175K in total profit for an Amazon store. My analysis identified that 48% of sales volume is driven by individual consumers and 43% of users prefer Cash on Delivery (COD). I implemented geographic mapping to show sales density across North America and tracked a high 95.14% order acceptance rate. The project highlights that Phones are the most profitable sub-category, contributing $0.20M to the store's total revenue. This dashboard allows for 360-degree monitoring of logistics performance and customer satisfaction metrics across multiple ship modes.
